How it works

From inbox to invoiced, without the chaos

FloTize connects your email, your team, and your clients into one intelligent workflow — powered by AI from the moment an enquiry arrives.

1

Enquiry arrives

An email lands in your monitored inbox. FloTize's AI reads it, classifies it, extracts the client's name and contact details, and creates a new request automatically — in under two minutes.

2

AI drafts your response

Based on your past replies, your knowledge base, and the client's message, FloTize drafts a suggested response. Edit it, personalise it, or send it as-is — your choice.

3

Track it through to won

Move requests through your pipeline — quoted, won, delivered. Log effort, track costs, generate invoices, and see exactly where every piece of work stands.

4

Analyse and improve

Revenue, margins, response times, conversion rates — all in one analytics dashboard. AI-powered insights surface what's working and what needs attention.
