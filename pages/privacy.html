Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

FloTize ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered workflow management platform for service businesses. Our contact email is micheal.sheehan@flotize.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and business details provided when signing up or contacting us
  • Usage data: Information about how you use the FloTize platform, including feature usage and login activity
  • Client data you upload: Information about your clients that you input or that is imported via email ingestion — including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and correspondence
  • Email content: Emails processed by the FloTize AI ingestion feature are read and analysed to create workflow requests
  • Technical data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information collected automatically

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the FloTize platform
  • To process client enquiries via AI email ingestion
  • To generate AI-powered responses and insights
  • To send service-related communications
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data storage and security

All data is stored in Microsoft Azure infrastructure in the North Europe (Ireland) region. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security practices and access controls to protect your data.

5. AI processing

FloTize uses Azure OpenAI (GPT-4o) to classify incoming emails, extract information, and generate suggested responses. Email content is processed by Azure OpenAI in accordance with Microsoft's data processing terms. We do not use your data to train AI models.

6. Your rights under GDPR

If you are based in the EU or UK, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@flotize.com.

7. Data retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active. Client data within the FloTize platform is retained according to the data retention policies you configure within the platform. Upon account cancellation, data is deleted within 90 days unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Third-party services

FloTize integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Azure — cloud infrastructure and storage
  • Microsoft Graph API — email integration
  • Azure OpenAI — AI processing

9. Cookies

The FloTize website uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at info@flotize.com.
