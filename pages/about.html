About FloTize

Built by people who understand
service businesses

FloTize started with a simple observation: service businesses were drowning in emails, losing leads, and spending hours on admin that should take minutes.

Our story

We built the tool we wished existed

Service businesses — solicitors, accountants, consultants, estate agents — run on client relationships. But the tools available to manage those relationships were either too complex, too generic, or simply not built with AI in mind.

We set out to build something different: a workflow management platform that uses AI to do the heavy lifting — capturing enquiries, drafting responses, monitoring SLAs, and surfacing insights — so that service professionals can spend their time on the work that actually matters to their clients.

FloTize is built on Microsoft Azure, uses GPT-4o for AI features, and integrates natively with Microsoft 365. It's designed to be set up in a day, not a month — and to pay for itself in the first week.
