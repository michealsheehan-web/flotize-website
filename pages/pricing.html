Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

We don't offer a self-serve free trial, but we do offer a free 30-minute demo where we'll show you exactly how FloTize would work for your business. Book a demo and if it's a good fit, we'll get you set up straight away.

Can I change plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Contact us at info@flotize.com and we'll sort it within one business day.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee for self-managed onboarding. If you'd like our team to handle the setup, configure your workflows, and train your staff, that's available as a professional services engagement from $1,500.

What email providers do you support?

FloTize currently integrates with Microsoft 365 and Outlook via Microsoft Graph API. Support for additional email providers is on our roadmap.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored in Microsoft Azure (North Europe region), encrypted at rest and in transit. We're GDPR-compliant and take data security seriously. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes — no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Contact us at info@flotize.com to cancel.
